Cookie Policy

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember information about your activity and preferences.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Session Cookies (Essential)

Type Purpose
_auth_token Maintain authenticated user session
_user_id Identify unique user
_csrf_token Protection against CSRF attacks
_session_id Maintain browsing session

2.2 Preference Cookies

Type Purpose
_lang Remember preferred language
_theme Remember theme preference (dark/light)
_settings Save user preferences

2.3 Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with SpinDraw:

  • _ga: Identifies unique users
  • _gid: Identifies sessions
  • _gat: Limits request rate

More information about Google cookies

2.4 Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

Google uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history:

  • NID: Stores advertising preference information
  • ANID: Identifies user for personalized advertising

You can control this at Google Ad Settings

2.5 Third-Party Cookies

Our site may contain cookies from:

  • Google: Analytics, AdSense, reCAPTCHA
  • Payment Providers: PayPhone, etc.
  • Social Networks: For social integration

3. Cookie Control

You can control and delete cookies in the following ways:

3.1 Browser Settings

You can adjust cookie settings in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

3.2 Do Not Track (DNT)

If your browser sends a DNT signal, we respect that preference for non-essential analytics.

3.3 Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Ads Preferences
  • NAI Opt-Out Tool
  • Digital Advertising Alliance

4. Essential vs. Non-Essential Cookies

Cookies are classified into two categories:

4.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Authentication and security
  • Session management
  • CSRF protection

4.2 Non-Essential Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience but are not required:

  • Analytics and performance
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Personalization features
